My Journey as a Data Scientist

I am Zahra, a junior data scientist. I have a Ph.D. in physical chemistry in the field of statistical thermodynamics, and during my thesis, I did my simulation codes with C and MATLAB. After graduation, I worked as a researcher and lecturer. Since I was interested in both programming and statistics and their application in data modeling, In 2021, I started learning machine learning seriously. I started with these two pieces of training on the udemy site

  • Python A-Z™: Python For Data Science With Real Exercises!
  • Machine Learning A-Z™: Python & R in Data Science

    After that in 2022, I completed a Data Science bootcamp at Jedha in Paris and my final project was to deployment an application to detect lung cancer from CT scan images.

    As a data scientist, my preference is to work on medical and health-related data.

    At the same time, I benefited from Andrew Ng 's very helpful training videos on Coursera. I also followed the educational videos of Seyed Naser RAZAVI , Alireza AkhavanPour and Morita Tarvirdians which taught me a lot.
